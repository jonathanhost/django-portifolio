About Me

I have 10 years of professional experience in the web hosting industry, specializing in technical support, infrastructure troubleshooting, and website hosting environments. Throughout my career, I have worked extensively diagnosing and resolving complex issues related to websites, servers, and hosting services, consistently delivering reliable solutions and maintaining high service quality.

I have strong hands-on experience with cPanel/WHM (certified), along with deep knowledge of domain management, DNS configuration, databases, email services, and the core technologies that power web hosting platforms.

My background also includes web development training and a development bootcamp, where I gained practical experience with PHP, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Python. This development knowledge helps me better understand application behavior and troubleshoot issues at both the application and server levels.

In addition, I have been expanding my expertise in Linux server administration and am currently studying for the LPIC-1 certification. My experience includes configuring and maintaining services such as Apache, MySQL, and PHP, as well as working with hosting control panels including CyberPanel, VestaCP, CentOS Web Panel, cPanel, and Webmin.

Working for a decade in the same company has allowed me to develop strong problem-solving skills, autonomy, and responsibility, often operating in environments that require quick diagnostics and effective solutions to ensure service availability and customer satisfaction.

I am passionate about Linux, hosting infrastructure, automation, and continuous learning, always seeking to deepen my technical expertise and contribute to stable and efficient systems.
